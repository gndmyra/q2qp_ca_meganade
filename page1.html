Initiatives

Outreach for Students Who Want to Join in the 1st Run of Likharal

The Likharal Organization's first campaign was done in March 15, 2023. It was an outreach or recruitment campaign directed to grade 5 students of public schools in Davao City who want to become scholars of the Philippine Science High School.

Donation Drive for Victims of A Fire in Punta Dumalag 1

In April 23, 2023, Likharal held a donation drive for the families who got affected by a fire that hit Punta Dumalag 1 in Barangay Matina Aplaya, Davao City due to unattended children playing with fire in April 21, 2023.

Recruitment of New Members for Likharal Committees

Before the start of another Likharal run, the organization had a call for applicants, particularly in Philippines Science High School Southern Mindanao Campus, who want to be part of Likharal.

Outreach for Students Who Want to Join in the 2nd Run of Likharal

In November 21, 2023, Likharal reached out to grade 5 or 4 aspiring scholars of the Philippine Science HIgh School.

Likharal and the Philippine Science High School - SMC

The whole Likharal Organization was made possible by the PSHS-SMC community. All the campaigns and sessions, including everything that were mentioned, were run alongside with the school; the management and personnel and the students, who worked hard and shared their time, talent, and resources to make this project happen.

Recent Activities

For future activities, Likharal plans to conduct more fun and quality sessions for the students joining the 2nd run.
