Vision

We strive to support public school students and foster them into reaching their full potential as future Iskolar ng Bayan and Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) leaders who will be of service to the community by making use of what they've learned.

Mission

Likharal aspires to provide access to resources that will help them achieve academic excellence and quality education. It intends to connect the students to opportunities that will elevate their knowledge and skills in English, Mathematics, and Science.
